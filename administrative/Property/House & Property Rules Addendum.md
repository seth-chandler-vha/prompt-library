# HOUSE & PROPERTY RULES ADDENDUM  
44724 Big Meadows Rd, Proctor, Montana 59929  

This House Rules / Property Rules Addendum is made part of the Residential Lease Agreement dated ____________, 2026, between Michael Kyle Chandler (“Landlord”) and Malcom Smith and Angie Smith (“Tenants”) for the Premises located at 44724 Big Meadows Rd, Proctor, Montana 59929.

These rules are adopted pursuant to Landlord’s authority to adopt rules under [MCA § 70‑24‑311](https://archive.legmt.gov/bills/mca/title_0700/chapter_0240/part_0030/section_0110/0700-0240-0030-0110.html) and are intended to promote safety, property protection, and peaceful enjoyment.

1. **Quiet Enjoyment.**  
   - Quiet hours are from **10:00 p.m. to 7:00 a.m.** daily. During these times, Tenants shall minimize noise that may disturb neighbors, including loud music, engines, or gatherings.

2. **Use of Outbuildings.**  
   - Any barn, shop, or other outbuildings on the property remain in Landlord’s exclusive possession and are **not** included in the leased Premises. Tenants shall not enter, use, or store personal property in these structures without Landlord’s written consent.

3. **Grounds and Yard.**  
   - Tenants shall keep yard areas free of excessive clutter, trash, or debris.  
   - Outdoor storage of loose materials (scrap, parts, junk items) is prohibited.  
   - Fire pits or open burning are allowed only if permitted by local regulations and with Landlord’s prior consent.

4. **Vehicles and Parking.**  
   - Parking is allowed only in designated driveway/parking areas.  
   - No parking on lawns, landscaped areas, or over the septic tank or drain field.  
   - No RVs, boats, trailers, or derelict/inoperable vehicles shall be stored on the Premises without Landlord’s prior written consent.

5. **Smoking and Vaping.**  
   - Smoking and vaping are prohibited inside the dwelling.  
   - If smoking is permitted outdoors, Tenants must use proper receptacles and prevent littering or fire hazards.

6. **Firearms and Hazardous Materials.**  
   - Lawful possession of firearms is permitted but must be stored safely and in compliance with all applicable laws.  
   - Tenants shall not store explosives, large quantities of flammable liquids, or hazardous chemicals on the Premises.

7. **Pets and Animals.**  
   - No pets are allowed without a signed pet addendum.  
   - Tenants shall not feed or attract wildlife (e.g., leaving food or garbage accessible to bears or other animals).

8. **Rule Changes.**  
   - Landlord may adopt reasonable changes to these rules by providing Tenants at least 30 days’ written notice, consistent with [MCA § 70‑24‑311](https://archive.legmt.gov/bills/mca/title_0700/chapter_0240/part_0030/section_0110/0700-0240-0030-0110.html). Any rule change will apply prospectively and may not substantially modify Tenants’ rights or obligations under the Lease.

In the event of a conflict between this Addendum and the Lease, the Lease controls unless the Addendum is more restrictive in a manner allowed by Montana law.
