# MOLD DISCLOSURE ADDENDUM  
44724 Big Meadows Rd, Proctor, Montana 59929  

This Mold Disclosure Addendum is made part of the Residential Lease Agreement dated ____________, 2026, between Michael Kyle Chandler (“Landlord”) and Malcom Smith and Angie Smith (“Tenants”) for the Premises located at 44724 Big Meadows Rd, Proctor, Montana 59929.

This disclosure is provided pursuant to [MCA § 70‑16‑703](https://archive.legmt.gov/bills/mca/title_0700/chapter_0160/part_0070/section_0030/0700-0160-0070-0030.html).

1. **Information Regarding Mold.** Mold is a naturally occurring organism that can grow in areas of excess moisture. Prolonged exposure to mold may cause health problems, especially for individuals with respiratory conditions, allergies, or compromised immune systems.

2. **Landlord’s Knowledge.** As of the date of this Addendum, Landlord has **no actual knowledge** of visible mold growth or mold‑related water intrusion in the Premises, other than any conditions specifically noted in the Move‑In Condition Statement.

3. **Tenant Duties Regarding Mold.** Tenants agree to:  
   - Keep the Premises clean and use ventilation fans in bathrooms and kitchens when available.  
   - Promptly notify Landlord in writing of any water leaks, persistent condensation, visible mold growth, or musty odors.  
   - Promptly dry any spills or standing water on floors, countertops, or other surfaces.

4. **Access for Inspection and Remediation.** Upon reasonable notice as required by [MCA § 70‑24‑312](https://archive.legmt.gov/bills/mca/title_0700/chapter_0240/part_0030/section_0120/0700-0240-0030-0120.html), Tenants agree to allow Landlord and any qualified professionals to enter the Premises to inspect for and remediate mold‑related conditions.

5. **No Warranty.** Landlord does not warrant that mold will never develop in the Premises. Tenants’ prompt reporting and cooperation are essential to addressing any moisture or mold concerns.

By signing below, Tenants acknowledge receipt of this Mold Disclosure Addendum and the opportunity to ask questions.
