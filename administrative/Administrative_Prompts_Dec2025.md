# Administrative Leadership Prompt Library
Deputy Chief of Staff - Montana VA Health Care System
Last Updated: December 2025

## Meeting Preparation

### Executive Meeting Briefing
Prepare briefing document for leadership meeting covering:
- Key agenda items with background
- Decision points requiring action
- Supporting data and metrics
- Stakeholder considerations
- Recommended positions

### Staff Meeting Agenda Generator
Create structured meeting agenda including:
- Welcome and safety moment
- Previous action item review
- Current priorities and updates
- Discussion topics with time allocations
- Action items and assignments

### Meeting Minutes Synthesizer
Transform meeting notes into professional minutes:
- Attendees and date/time
- Key discussion points
- Decisions made and rationale
- Action items with owners and deadlines
- Follow-up required

## Quality Improvement

### Quality Metrics Dashboard Analysis
Analyze quality performance data:
- Trend identification and significance
- Comparison to national VA benchmarks
- Root cause analysis framework
- Improvement recommendations
- Implementation timeline

### Patient Safety Event Review
Structure patient safety event analysis:
- Event timeline and contributing factors
- Systems analysis (not individual blame)
- Corrective action plan
- Prevention strategies
- Monitoring plan

### SAIL/SHEP Score Interpretation
Interpret Strategic Analytics for Improvement and Learning scores:
- Score breakdown by domain
- Comparison to peer facilities
- Improvement opportunities
- Action plan development

## Policy & Compliance

### Policy Review Template
Review VA policy or directive:
- Summary of key requirements
- Impact on current operations
- Implementation steps needed
- Resource requirements
- Compliance monitoring plan

### Credentialing Documentation
Generate credentialing and privileging documents:
- Provider qualification summary
- Scope of practice definition
- Competency verification
- Ongoing monitoring plan
- Reappointment criteria

## Communication

### Leadership Communication Draft
Draft communication for staff/leadership:
- Clear purpose statement
- Key messages (3-5 points)
- Context and background
- Expected actions or responses
- Professional and empathetic tone

### Email Response Templates
Generate professional responses for:
- Information requests
- Complaint acknowledgment and resolution
- Policy clarification
- Meeting invitations/declines
- Recognition and appreciation

## Strategic Planning

### SWOT Analysis Framework
Conduct SWOT analysis for:
- Current state assessment
- Opportunity identification
- Threat mitigation strategies
- Strategic initiative prioritization

### Resource Justification
Build business case for resources:
- Problem statement with data
- Proposed solution details
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Risk assessment
- Implementation timeline
- Success metrics

## Custom Instructions for Administrative Space

```
You are supporting the Deputy Chief of Staff for Montana VA Health Care 
System in administrative and leadership functions.

When handling administrative prompts:
1. Follow VA policy hierarchy (VHA Directive > Handbook > Memorandum)
2. Consider both clinical excellence and operational efficiency
3. Maintain professional tone appropriate for executive communication
4. Include data-driven justification for recommendations
5. Consider stakeholder perspectives (clinical staff, leadership, patients)
6. Reference applicable regulatory requirements (Joint Commission, VHA)

For quality improvement work:
- Use established QI frameworks (PDSA, Lean, Six Sigma)
- Focus on system-level factors, not individual performance
- Include measurable outcomes and timelines
- Consider sustainability and spread potential

For communication:
- Be clear, concise, and actionable
- Use appropriate formality for audience
- Anticipate questions and address proactively
```
